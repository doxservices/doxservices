Frequently Asked Questions

Short answers. No fluff.

Do you negotiate on price?
Our cars are priced to market using real‑time comps. If a unit sits, we lower it. No hidden fees.
How fast is financing?
Same‑day in most cases. Bring ID, proof of income, and address. Pre‑approval takes ~5 minutes online.
Trade‑in process?
Instant appraisal after a quick inspection. Use it as down payment or take cash.
What inspections do you perform?
180‑point checklist: powertrain, chassis, electronics, fluids, brakes, tires, and road test.
